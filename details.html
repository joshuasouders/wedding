Event Details

Questions? Contact us at josh@joshandkatesouders.com or kate@joshandkatesouders.com.

The ceremony and reception will be held at Patapsco State Park, Hollofield Area (8020 Baltimore National Pike, Ellicott City, MD 21043) on Saturday May 16th, 2015. The ceremony will begin at noon and the reception will conclude at 5:00 PM. Tell the gate attendant that you are here for the wedding in Shelter #300 in order to avoid having to pay to get into the park. After entering the park, drive straight past the gate attendant. The road will come to a Y; go right, and it is the first shelter on your right after the Y. There is parking right in front of the shelter, but if it is all taken there are more parking spaces if you keep driving past the shelter.
